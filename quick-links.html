Official Government Portals

All the Portals You Need, in One Place

Click any card to open the official government portal directly. Need help with filing or registration? Our team handles everything end-to-end.

Income Tax

Official Income Tax e-Filing portal for ITR filing, refunds, Form 26AS, AIS, TDS certificates, and tax payments.

incometax.gov.in Open Portal
GST

Goods and Services Tax Network portal — GST registration, return filing (GSTR-1, 3B, 9), ITC reconciliation, and payment.

services.gst.gov.in Open Portal
MCA / ROC

Ministry of Corporate Affairs portal — company and LLP registration, annual filings, director details, and MCA21 services.

mca.gov.in Open Portal
PF / EPFO

Employees' Provident Fund Organisation — UAN activation, PF balance, ECR filing, PF withdrawal, and employer services.

epfindia.gov.in Open Portal
ESIC

Employees' State Insurance Corporation — ESIC registration, monthly contribution, IP card, claim status, and employer portal.

esic.gov.in Open Portal
AP Professional Tax

Andhra Pradesh Professional Tax portal — employer registration, PT challan payment, and return filing for AP businesses.

appt.gov.in Open Portal

All links above open official Government of India portals in a new tab. Sivasurya Associates is not affiliated with any government body. If you need help navigating these portals — registration, filing, payments, or resolving errors — contact us for assistance.
